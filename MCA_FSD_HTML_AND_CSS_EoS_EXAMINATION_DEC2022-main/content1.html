Goneril is by far the easiest of the three sisters, which you find at the most northeast point of the Grantebridgescire region, in a place called Spalda Fens.

The fight involves her using some teleporting mechanics and poison gas, but nothing too crazy until her HP bar drops to half.

That's when she starts adding some charged AoE attacks that will hit hard if you don't get away from the circle quickly enough.

Daughters of Lerion in Assassin's Creed:

To get three of Thor's gear pieces - the Battle Breeches, Plate and Gauntlets - you need to defeat the Daughters of Lerion and retrieve their daggers. These three boss battles differ in power level, so you will be able to defeat them at different points of the game.

The three sisters reside not very far from each other, but perhaps the most distinctive sign to find them is that they live in a limited open space crowned by a tied corpse that you must interact with to begin the battle.

Here's when the fights start to get trickier, so you need to start thinking about skill combinations. It's strongly advised to use the 'Brush with Death' skill to slow the time when you dodge, creating openings to attack. This works exceptionally in combination with 'Backstab' and 'Grit' since the first skill makes you inflict more damage from behind and the second skill allows you to heal the red portion of the HP bar.
